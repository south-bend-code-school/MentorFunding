About Us

Converting student debt from a burden to a benefit

Student debt has a profound impact on the daily lives of many young Americans, preventing individuals from buying a home, starting a family, and saving for retirement and other investments. Student loan debt is stagnating the growth of young adults, however, refinancing student debt can change this, avoiding the unreasonable delay of significant life goals and purposes.

Mentor Fundings platform provides secure student loan refinancing; coupling low interest rates and flexible repayment terms with unmatched career progression mentorship.

Mentor Fundings mission is to become the best-in-class student loan refinancing and career mentorship service in the development of data-driven solutions to alleviate the burdens of debt, improve professional success, and facilitate financial independence.

Our goals are to:

  • Alleviate the burdens of student debt, which are stagnating the growth of many young Americans, and postponing major life accomplishments;

  • Provide career-progression and development mentorship to young professionals from trusted, connected sources vested in their success;

  • Enable individuals to earn greater returns, increase savings, and eliminate periodic expenditures;

  • Help you declare your financial freedom!

WHY REFINANCE?

WHY NOW?

Student loan refinancing could help you take advantage of your decreased credit risk by locking in a lower interest rate.

Refinancing your student loans can lower your monthly payments; ideal for grads who do not qualify for income-based repayment but do not make enough money yet to comfortably make their student loan payments.

Student loan refinancing allows you to consolidate your multiple student loans into one monthly payment, making repayment easier to organize, track, and repay.

Refinancing provides you with the opportunity to switch to a new financial servicer provider that cares truly cares about you and has your best interest at heart. A service like Mentor Funding!
